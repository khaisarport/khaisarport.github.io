About Me

I am Muhammad Khaisar Ardhana, I am a graduate of SMK Negeri 62 Jakarta majoring in Multimedia. I have some expertise in the field of multimedia, namely graphic design, motion graphics, video editing, and a little bit of web designing. During school I was active in multimedia activities at school, among others, I was assigned to create poster designs for school activities, banner designs for school activities, make logos for school hotels, and be part of a video editor for school commercial advertising needs and school activities.

In addition, I have experience in PKL(Internship) programs in two different places. For the first place I underwent the internship, I did it at the Ministry of Agriculture of the Republic of Indonesia in South Jakarta, where I worked as a graphic designer who focused on making statistical designs for agricultural development in Indonesia. For the second place, I did my internship at NR Productions in Bogor, where I acted as a graphic designer for print and digital media purposes, such as creating designs for posters, banners, labels, packaging, infographics, instagram feeds and motion graphic assets.
